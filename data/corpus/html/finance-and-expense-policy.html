Owner: Finance
Version: 3.0-rag

1. Purpose and Scope

Finance and Expense Policy describes the formal policy requirements and operating procedures used by Northstar Analytics Ltd.. It is written as a synthetic but realistic document for training retrieval, summarization, and question-answering systems.

This document applies to all employees, interns, temporary workers, and approved contractors unless a section explicitly states otherwise.

Managers are responsible for enforcing this policy consistently and for escalating unusual cases to the document owner.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

Employees must read, understand, and follow the policy requirements relevant to their role.

Managers must document exceptions in writing and retain approvals in the designated system of record.

The document owner reviews this policy at least annually and whenever business, legal, or technical requirements materially change.

3. Expense Categories

The section on expense categories defines the standard rule, the approval path, and the records that must be retained.

Requests related to expense categories should be submitted through the official workflow rather than handled informally through chat or email alone.

When a manager grants an exception for expense categories, the exception must include the reason, the duration, and any compensating controls.

Meals above USD 75 per person require an itemized receipt and business purpose note.

Home internet reimbursement is capped at USD 50 per month for approved remote roles.
