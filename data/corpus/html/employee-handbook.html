1. Purpose and Scope

Employee Handbook describes the formal policy requirements and operating procedures used by Northstar Analytics Ltd.. It is written as a synthetic but realistic document for training retrieval, summarization, and question-answering systems.

This document applies to all employees, interns, temporary workers, and approved contractors unless a section explicitly states otherwise.

Managers are responsible for enforcing this policy consistently and for escalating unusual cases to the document owner.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

Employees must read, understand, and follow the policy requirements relevant to their role.

Managers must document exceptions in writing and retain approvals in the designated system of record.

The document owner reviews this policy at least annually and whenever business, legal, or technical requirements materially change.

3. Employment Classifications

The section on employment classifications defines the standard rule, the approval path, and the records that must be retained.

Requests related to employment classifications should be submitted through the official workflow rather than handled informally through chat or email alone.

When a manager grants an exception for employment classifications, the exception must include the reason, the duration, and any compensating controls.

Records related to employment classifications must be stored in the designated repository with a clear owner and retention period.

Metrics related to employment classifications should be reviewed periodically to identify recurring issues and opportunities for improvement.

4. Attendance

Employees who will be absent or late must notify their manager and team channel at least 1 hour before the start of the scheduled workday unless there is an emergency.

Unplanned absences extending beyond 3 consecutive workdays may require supporting documentation.

6. Travel And Expenses

The section on travel and expenses defines the standard rule, the approval path, and the records that must be retained.
