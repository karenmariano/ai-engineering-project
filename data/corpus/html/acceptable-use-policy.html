Owner: Security

Effective date: 2026-04-20

1. Purpose and Scope

Acceptable Use Policy describes the formal policy requirements and operating procedures used by Northstar Analytics Ltd.. It is written as a synthetic but realistic document for training retrieval, summarization, and question-answering systems.

This document applies to all employees, interns, temporary workers, and approved contractors unless a section explicitly states otherwise.

Managers are responsible for enforcing this policy consistently and for escalating unusual cases to the document owner.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

Employees must read, understand, and follow the policy requirements relevant to their role.

Managers must document exceptions in writing and retain approvals in the designated system of record.

The document owner reviews this policy at least annually and whenever business, legal, or technical requirements materially change.

3. Approved Use

The section on approved use defines the standard rule, the approval path, and the records that must be retained.

Requests related to approved use should be submitted through the official workflow rather than handled informally through chat or email alone.

When a manager grants an exception for approved use, the exception must include the reason, the duration, and any compensating controls.

Company systems are primarily for legitimate business use, with only limited incidental personal use that does not interfere with work or security.

Users must protect confidential information when using collaboration tools, file sharing platforms, and AI systems.
