## 1. Purpose and Scope

Risk Management Policy describes the formal policy requirements and operating procedures used by Northstar Analytics Ltd.. It is written as a synthetic but realistic document for training retrieval, summarization, and question-answering systems.

This document applies to all employees, interns, temporary workers, and approved contractors unless a section explicitly states otherwise.

Managers are responsible for enforcing this policy consistently and for escalating unusual cases to the document owner.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

Employees must read, understand, and follow the policy requirements relevant to their role.

Risks are scored on a 1 to 5 scale for likelihood and impact, and inherent risk is calculated by multiplying the two values.

Any risk with an inherent score of 15 or higher must be reviewed by the executive risk committee.
