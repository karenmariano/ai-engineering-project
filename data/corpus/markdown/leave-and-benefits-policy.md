## 1. Purpose and Scope

Leave and Benefits Policy describes the formal policy requirements and operating procedures used by Northstar Analytics Ltd.. It is written as a synthetic but realistic document for training retrieval, summarization, and question-answering systems.

This document applies to all employees, interns, temporary workers, and approved contractors unless a section explicitly states otherwise.

Managers are responsible for enforcing this policy consistently and for escalating unusual cases to the document owner.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities

Employees must read, understand, and follow the policy requirements relevant to their role.

Managers must document exceptions in writing and retain approvals in the designated system of record.

The document owner reviews this policy at least annually and whenever business, legal, or technical requirements materially change.

## 3. Annual Leave

The section on annual leave defines the standard rule, the approval path, and the records that must be retained.

Requests related to annual leave should be submitted through the official workflow rather than handled informally through chat or email alone.

When a manager grants an exception for annual leave, the exception must include the reason, the duration, and any compensating controls.

Full-time employees accrue 20 working days of annual leave per year, prorated for partial years of service.

Leave requests of more than 10 consecutive working days require manager approval at least 2 weeks in advance.
